Hi Information team,

Not pitching a product placement — pitching a story idea.

By 2026 a meaningful share of pageviews on commerce and docs sites come from agents (Claude browsing, ChatGPT search, Perplexity, internal RPA). They re-read HTML designed for humans, burning tokens to skim past nav, hydration scripts, and CSS-in-JS. There is no equivalent of RSS — the small, lossy, machine-first feed that made the blog web legible to a different reader.

A few of us are trying to build that layer. I shipped AHTML (https://ahtml.dev) — one of several attempts; llmstxt.org and Firecrawl are adjacent shots at the same problem from different angles.
